Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our website and how we use it. We only collect information that helps us provide you with our services and keep you updated.

When you sign up for our newsletter, we collect your email address and, if provided, your name. This information is used to send you updates, news, and promotional content about Escape the Matrix Group. This newsletter service is managed through MailerLite, a trusted third-party platform that securely handles your subscription data.

If you choose to contact us through our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the message you send. This information is used to respond to your inquiries and to communicate with you about your questions or comments. Your contact form submissions are securely processed and stored using Google Apps Script and Google Sheets, which helps us organize and track your messages.

The information we collect (your name and email for newsletter and contact) is used for marketing purposes, to improve our services, and to communicate relevant information about Escape the Matrix Group directly to you. We do not share or sell your personal information to outside parties for their independent marketing or any other use. We are committed to keeping your information safe and using it responsibly to enhance your experience with us.
